Dune 3 trailer release

Dune: Part Three concludes Denis Villeneuve’s epic vision, adapting Frank Herbert’s Dune Messiah. Set 12 years after Part Two, it follows Paul Atreides as Emperor, facing political intrigue, shifting alliances, and mounting unrest that challenges his rule and legacy. With its stellar cast, sweeping visuals, and Villeneuve’s meticulous storytelling, early buzz suggests it will be a deeply ambitious, thought‑provoking finale and a must‑see for sci‑fi fans.

Director: Denis Villeneuve

Based on: Dune Messiah
